# AgriVida v1.0 Release Notes

**Released:** April 2026

---

## What AgriVida Does

AgriVida coordinates surplus food and compost redistribution between distribution hubs, local farmers, and drivers. Hub managers plan routes. Farmers get notified by text. Drivers track deliveries. No spreadsheets. No app downloads for farmers. Just coordination that works.

---

## What's New in v1.0

### Route Planning & Publishing

Hub managers can build delivery routes on an interactive map, add stops, and automatically notify nearby farmers — all from one screen.

- **Interactive map planning** with address autocomplete
- **Dynamic stops** — add, remove, and reorder waypoints
- **Nearby farmer matching** — see which farmers are within 10 miles as you build
- **One-click publishing** — SMS notifications sent automatically to matched farmers
- **Real-time response tracking** — see farmer confirmations as they come in

### SMS-First Farmer Engagement

Farmers don't log in. They don't download apps. They receive a text message with the route name, pickup window, and a one-tap link to respond. Options: **Crop Pickup**, **Compost Pickup**, or **Both**. One-tap unsubscribe included.

This keeps the barrier to entry as low as possible — critical for reaching farmers in rural and underserved areas who may not have smartphones or reliable data connectivity.

### Driver Portal

Drivers see assigned routes, update status through the delivery lifecycle, and upload quality check photos from the field.

- **Assignment timeline** with pickup windows and destinations
- **Status updates** — Waiting → Started → In Progress → Completed
- **Photo uploads** for delivery verification
- **Route and contact details** per assignment

### AI Load Capacity Estimation

Hub managers can estimate remaining truck capacity from a single photo.

- **Photo upload** of the truck bed
- **Dimension input** (length, width, height in feet)
- **Instant analysis** — fill percentage, floor coverage, fit assessment
- **Confidence scoring** and safety notes

This helps prevent underloaded trucks, reduces unnecessary trips, and improves overall fleet efficiency.

### Hub Operations Dashboard

A central command center for hub managers.

- **Overview stats** — nearby growers, pending requests, active trips
- **Quick route creation**
- **Active routes board**
- **Integrated load capacity estimator**

### Farmer Directory

Browse registered farmers, view locations and contact info, and see interaction history including past responses and SMS delivery status.

### Compost Pickup Support

Farmers can request compost pickup through the same SMS response flow used for crop surplus.

### Theme Support

Toggle between light and dark modes. Preference persists automatically.

## By Role

### For Hub Managers

- Plan and publish routes with interactive maps
- Automatically match and notify nearby farmers via SMS
- Assign drivers and track assignment status
- Monitor route responses in real time
- Estimate truck load capacity with AI
- View hub-level operational stats

### For Farmers

- Receive SMS route alerts with pickup opportunities
- Respond with one tap — no login or app required
- Request crop pickup, compost pickup, or both
- Unsubscribe from notifications at any time

### For Drivers

- View assigned routes and delivery details
- Update route status as deliveries progress
- Upload quality check photos from the field

---

## What's Next

The next development cycle will focus on **integration with existing distributor systems**. Many potential hub partners already use warehouse management, inventory, or routing software. Rather than forcing them onto a new platform, AgriVida will meet them where they are — via APIs, data imports, and embeddable coordination tools.
